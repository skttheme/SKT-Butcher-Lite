SKT-Butcher-Lite

Butcher Lite is a simple and clean WordPress theme made for food and meat-related websites. It works well as a [Free Meat Shop WordPress Theme](https://www.sktthemes.org/shop/free-meat-shop-wordpress-theme/) for local meat stores, fresh food sellers, and small butcher shops that need an easy online presence. The layout is clear and easy to read.

This theme can also be used as a Butcher Shop WordPress Theme for traditional meat markets, meat suppliers, and fresh food services. You can share product details, prices, and contact information without confusion.

Butcher Lite also fits as a [Poultry Farm WordPress Theme](https://www.sktthemes.org/shop/poultry-farm-wordpress-theme/) for chicken farms, egg suppliers, and poultry businesses. It is beginner-friendly, responsive, and helps create a trustworthy website using simple language and a clean design.
